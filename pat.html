PERUNTHENARUVI FALLS

A natural waterfall with captivating surroundings is situated on the banks of the River Pamba. The fall is 60 to 100 ft deep which pours down on a rocky bed.

GAVI

Gavi is situated some 14 km away from the famed Periyar Tiger Reserve and is heaven on Earth, quite literally

THRIVENI SANGAMAM

As the name suggests this place is famous for the meeting point of the three rivers. It serves as the halting point for those on their way to Sabarimala.

KONNI FOREST RESERVE

This place is considered to be the equivalent to an elephant's cage. The reason being is that the main attraction of the region is a massive wooden cage where elephant are trained..

KAVIYOOR ROCK TEMPLE

Kaviyoor Rock Temple is the only cave temple left in the state which glorifies the culture of the area more that its spiritual aspecthis ancient cave temple established in Tamil Pallava style is devoted to Lord Hanuman, the Monkey God. .

MANJINIKARA CHURCH

Another significant Syrian Christian's pilgrim center, Manjinikkara Church is placed in Manjanikkara on the top of a hill about 6 km from Pathanamthitta.

MULOOR SMARAKAM

Muloor Smarakam is a memorial dedicated to a poet and a pre-independence era social reformer Muloor S Padmanabha Panicker (1869-1931). Situated at a small distance of 12 km from Pathanamthitta town

SABARIMALA

Sabarimala houses Sastha Temple situated about 80 km from Pathanamthitta town. Dedicated to Lord Ayyappa, this temple is situated in the lap of nature amid the dangerous hills of Western Ghats.
