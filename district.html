Kerala

Kerala’s unhurried pace of life is evident across its inland waterways, spice plantations, forested hills and mustard-yellow beaches. The architecture harks back to the Portuguese, Dutch and British colonialists who fought to take charge, each leaving their mark. And, Keralan cooking is inspired by the coconuts and spices that grow here, and seafood from Kerala’s western Malabar Coast. This southern state proves a gentle introduction to India for first-timers, or a contrast if you’ve already visited the north
