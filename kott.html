NADUKANI

After all the blessings from temples, it is time for some adventure. Nadukani is one such place nestled amid the wilderness of nature and densely wooded hills offering its visitors a beautiful trail for trekking.

KOTTATHAVALAM

A fascinating cave carved out of rock surface is Kottathavalam lying on the top of a Murugan Hills at Kurisumala, considered to be the resting place for Madura Royal family and adorned with various sculptures of Gods and goddesses along with weapons used in the ancient times

THIRUNAKKARA TEMPLE

A popular Hindu shrine and great epitome of Kerala style of architecture, Thirunakkara Mahadev Temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva established in 16th century by the king of Thekkumkur.

KUMARAKOM BIRD SANCTUARY

A paradise for bird watchers, Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary located near vembanad lake spread 14 acres with 140 species of local and migratory birds.

ILLIKAL KALLU

Located at a height of 6000 ft above the sea level in the Western Ghats of Kottayam, Kerala, Illikkal Kallu is a major tourist attraction and one of the most pristine spots in all of India. Situated in Moonnilavu Village, Meenachil taluk

MARMALA WATERFALLS

Located at a distance of few kms from Erattupetta in Kottayam inside a private rubber estate, Marmala Waterfalls are popularly known as the ‘enchantress of the jungle’. Plummeting from a height of 200 feet.

WAGAMON

Vagamon or Wagamon is a hill station located in the Central Travancore region of Kerala, India. It is a warm and friendly place with cool climate as the temperature between 10° and 23°C during a summer midday. It is situated 1,100 m above sea leve

VAIKOM

It is the oldest city in Kottayam and lies very close to the famous tourist spot of Kumarakom. Vaikom Temple lies at the heart of the town and becomes very prominent during its Vaikom Ashtami celebrations during the month of November.
